Woodbridge Lakes is a grouping of row town-homes comprised of 92 units located just off the north side of Forest Hill Blvd. west of Congress Avenue and east of Military Trail in West Palm Beach, Florida. Most of the units overlook two large lakes located in the interior of the circle of homes. There is a clubhouse, a swimming pool, a bar-b-Que pit, newly refurbished tennis courts and a walking trail for the comfort and convenience of our owners, residents and their guests.  Please feel free to view the pages at this web site to learn more about our community and the people who live here.
